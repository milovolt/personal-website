My name's Emilou Flores.

I was born and raised in Pangasinan, Philippines. I moved to Quezon City for college. I earned my bachelor's in Electrical Engineering in the University of the Philippines - Diliman where I am also currently taking my master's degree. I am now working as a technical consultant and software developer in PowerSolv, Inc. where I also worked as a student assistant during my undergraduate years. I specialize in power system simulations such as load flow study, short-circuit, state estimation, reliability analysis, and harmonics analysis. You may also find me trying out a lot of things like painting, piano, table tennis, comics-making, frisbee, web designing, volleyball...the list goes on...
